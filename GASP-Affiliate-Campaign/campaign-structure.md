# Affiliate Campaign Structure & Workflow

## Phase 1: Lead Generation (Free Masterclass)

### Entry Point
- **Landing Page**: "Get Pregnant Naturally Masterclass"
- **Value Prop**: Free 60-minute masterclass with Dr. Nashat Latib
- **CTA**: "Reserve My Free Spot"
- **Lead Magnet**: Fertility checklist or quiz

### Email Sequence 1: Pre-Masterclass (3-5 emails)
1. **Welcome Email** (Immediate)
   - Confirm registration
   - Add to calendar
   - Share social proof

2. **Value Email** (Day 2)
   - Preview of masterclass content
   - Success stories
   - What to expect

3. **Reminder Email** (Day before)
   - Final reminder
   - Technical setup
   - Q&A opportunity

4. **Last Chance** (2 hours before)
   - Urgency
   - Limited spots
   - Mobile-friendly link

## Automation Tools Needed

### 1. Email Marketing Platform
- **Recommended**: ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, or Mailchimp
- **Features**: 
  - Tag-based automation
  - A/B testing
  - Analytics
  - Integration capabilities

### 2. Landing Page Builder
- **Options**: 
  - Custom HTML/CSS
  - ClickFunnels
  - LeadPages
  - Unbounce

### 3. Tracking & Analytics
- **Google Analytics**: Traffic sources
- **UTM Parameters**: Affiliate tracking
- **Conversion tracking**: Course sales
- **Lead scoring**: Quality assessment

### 4. Social Media Management
- **Buffer/Hootsuite**: Scheduled posts
- **Canva**: Design templates
- **Later**: Instagram automation
