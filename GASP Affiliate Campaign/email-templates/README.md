# Email Automation Templates for Dr. Nashat Latib's Fertility Masterclass

## Email Sequence Overview

### Phase 1: Pre-Masterclass Sequence (3-5 emails)
1. **Welcome Email** (Immediate)
2. **Value Email** (Day 2)
3. **Reminder Email** (Day before)
4. **Last Chance** (2 hours before)

### Phase 2: Post-Masterclass Sequence (7-14 emails)
1. **Thank You + Replay** (Immediate)
2. **Course Promotion** (Day 1)
3. **Success Story** (Day 3)
4. **FAQ + Benefits** (Day 5)
5. **Limited Time Offer** (Day 7)
6. **Final Reminder** (Day 10)
7. **Last Chance** (Day 14)

## Email Platform Integration

### Recommended Platforms:
- **ConvertKit** - Best for creators and course launches
- **ActiveCampaign** - Advanced automation and segmentation
- **Mailchimp** - User-friendly with good templates
- **Klaviyo** - E-commerce focused but great for courses

### Setup Requirements:
1. **API Integration** - Connect landing page to email platform
2. **Tagging System** - Track subscriber journey
3. **A/B Testing** - Optimize subject lines and content
4. **Analytics** - Monitor open rates, click rates, conversions

## Template Structure

Each email template includes:
- **Subject Line** (with A/B test variations)
- **Preheader Text** (email preview)
- **Header** (logo and branding)
- **Main Content** (value-focused copy)
- **Call-to-Action** (clear next step)
- **Footer** (unsubscribe, contact info)
- **Tracking Parameters** (UTM tags for analytics)

## Personalization Variables

### Basic Variables:
- `{first_name}` - Subscriber's first name
- `{email}` - Subscriber's email address
- `{timezone}` - Selected timezone
- `{affiliate_id}` - Referring affiliate

### Advanced Variables:
- `{days_since_signup}` - Days since registration
- `{masterclass_date}` - Scheduled masterclass date
- `{spots_remaining}` - Available spots
- `{course_price}` - Course pricing
- `{bonus_value}` - Bonus offer value

## Segmentation Strategy

### By Engagement Level:
- **High Engagers** - Opened 3+ emails, clicked links
- **Medium Engagers** - Opened 1-2 emails
- **Low Engagers** - No opens or clicks

### By Source:
- **Facebook Ads** - Different messaging for paid traffic
- **Organic Social** - Community-focused content
- **Affiliate Traffic** - Acknowledge referral source
- **Direct Traffic** - General audience messaging

## Automation Triggers

### Time-Based Triggers:
- **Immediate** - Welcome and confirmation emails
- **Delayed** - Reminder and follow-up sequences
- **Scheduled** - Masterclass day emails

### Behavior-Based Triggers:
- **Email Opens** - Send additional value content
- **Link Clicks** - Course promotion emails
- **Form Submissions** - Thank you and next steps
- **Page Visits** - Retargeting sequences

## Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track:
- **Open Rate** - Target: 25-35%
- **Click Rate** - Target: 3-5%
- **Conversion Rate** - Target: 1-3%
- **Unsubscribe Rate** - Target: <1%
- **Revenue per Email** - Track course sales

### Optimization Strategy:
1. **A/B Test Subject Lines** - Test 2-3 variations
2. **Test Send Times** - Find optimal delivery times
3. **Optimize Content** - Based on click patterns
4. **Segment Lists** - Send targeted content
5. **Clean Lists** - Remove inactive subscribers

## Compliance & Best Practices

### CAN-SPAM Compliance:
- Clear sender identification
- Accurate subject lines
- Physical address in footer
- Easy unsubscribe option
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days

### GDPR Compliance (if applicable):
- Clear consent language
- Data processing information
- Right to be forgotten
- Data portability options

### Email Best Practices:
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Alt text for images
- Plain text version
- Consistent branding
- Personalization
- Value-focused content
