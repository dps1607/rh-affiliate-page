## ðŸŽ¨ **Platform-Specific Outreach Strategies**

### **Instagram Strategy**
1. **Engage first**: Like and comment on their recent posts
2. **Follow them**: Show genuine interest in their content
3. **Send DM**: Use personalized template
4. **Follow up**: If no response, send a follow-up in 3-5 days

### **TikTok Strategy**
1. **Engage with content**: Like, comment, share their videos
2. **Follow them**: Build relationship first
3. **Send DM**: Use TikTok-specific template
4. **Offer collaboration**: Suggest video collaboration ideas

### **Email Strategy**
1. **Research**: Find their email address
2. **Personalize**: Mention specific content they've created
3. **Provide value**: Explain benefits for their audience
4. **Follow up**: Send 2-3 follow-ups over 2 weeks

### **LinkedIn Strategy**
1. **Connect first**: Send personalized connection request
2. **Engage**: Like and comment on their posts
3. **Send message**: Use professional template
4. **Follow up**: Professional follow-up messages
